Our Standards

What we check before publishing.

Primary source Links to the original paper, not secondary coverage
Effect size Cohen's d or equivalent always reported
Sample context Who was studied and how many
Replication status Noted when a finding has failed to reproduce
No pop inflation We do not repackage weak correlations as life-changing
No ad incentives Not funded by supplements or coaching programs
