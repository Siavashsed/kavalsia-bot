Why We Exist

Built to Replace
Gym-Bro Guesswork

Most training advice online is recycled opinion dressed up as expertise. A trend goes viral, gets repeated a thousand times, and suddenly it is treated as fact. The athlete loses, because the actual research rarely makes it into the feed.

SportIQ Pro started with a simple frustration: there was no single place to find training, recovery, and nutrition guidance that was both grounded in peer-reviewed evidence and written to be used. The studies existed. The clear, honest translation did not.

So we built it. Every guide we publish starts in the literature and ends as a protocol with sets, reps, timing, and a way to measure whether it worked. No magic supplements, no 30-day transformation promises, just systems that compound over months and years.
