About AI Marketing Pro

Built by a marketer who got tired of AI hype.

AI Marketing Pro exists to answer one question honestly: what do AI tools actually do for marketing performance when you measure them with real ad spend?

Why this site exists

Tested, not demoed.

Every week a new AI tool launches with a polished landing page, a vendor case study, and a sandbox demo that looks incredible. None of that tells you whether it moves ROAS on a live campaign.

AI Marketing Pro was started because that gap kept costing money. The site does one thing: it runs AI marketing tools through real campaigns with real budgets, then reports the before-and-after metrics accurately. Tools get named. Numbers get attached. If something underperforms, that gets said too.

No affiliate-driven rankings. No "10x your output" promises. Just the engineering reality of where AI saves time, where it improves conversion, and where it quietly adds revision overhead.
