About TradingTech Review

Reviews you can trade on.

TradingTech Review benchmarks trading platforms, APIs, and Python libraries in real production conditions - real capital, real slippage, real failure modes - and publishes exactly what broke.

Why This Site Exists

The review you read was written by the company selling it.

Most reviews of trading tools are vendor marketing in a different font. Demo-mode screenshots, affiliate-driven rankings, and "it works great" with no numbers attached. The reviewer rarely traded a live dollar through the thing.

I learned that the expensive way - shipping a strategy that backtested beautifully and bled capital in week one. The tool was not broken. The review was. So I started benchmarking everything myself, in production, and writing down what actually happened.

TradingTech Review is the resource I needed and could not find: every tool run with real money, fees and slippage on, then documented in full - benchmarks, code, and failure modes included.

The Method

How a tool gets reviewed.

01

The tool is set up the way a real desk would use it - live data, real account, full configuration.

02

Run With Real Capital

Strategies execute with actual money, fees and slippage on, so the numbers reflect production, not theory.

03

Benchmark & Break

Latency, fill quality, and edge cases get measured until the failure modes surface and get logged.
