The crypto media problem

Most crypto content is engineered to make you trade. It runs on engagement: bold predictions, fear, FOMO, and affiliate links to exchanges. The incentive is attention, not accuracy - and that costs readers real money.

CryptoPulse was built as the opposite of that. It is independent, unsponsored, and conflict-free. The analysis follows the data: on-chain flows, holder behavior, liquidity, and volatility regimes. When the evidence is inconclusive, that is what gets published.
