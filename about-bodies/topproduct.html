Why We Exist

The Viral Economy Was Built to Empty Your Wallet

A single 15-second clip can turn an unknown gadget into a global obsession overnight. The trouble is that going viral has nothing to do with being good. Most "TikTok made me buy it" products are generic items pulled from the same overseas catalogs, marked up three to five times, and shipped slowly in plain packaging.

We got tired of friends asking "is this thing actually good?" and finding no honest answer online - only affiliate roundups and reviews written by the seller. So we built TopProduct to break that cycle: a place where every verdict is earned through real money spent and real time used.

When a viral find is genuinely excellent, we are the first to say so. When it is recycled hype, we save you the disappointment and the return shipping.

Our Standards

The Rules Every Review Follows

Six principles guide every verdict we publish - and every product we tell you to skip.

Bought, Not Gifted

Every product is purchased at retail price like a normal shopper. Brands never get to send a polished sample and skew the result.

Tested Over Time

A first impression means nothing. We use products for a month or more so we catch the cheap hinge, the fading battery, the peeling coating.

Price Reality Checks

We trace viral products to their source listings, so you know if that $49 gadget is really a $12 item with a marketing budget.

Clear Verdicts

No vague star ratings. Every review ends with a plain answer: buy it, skip it, or wait for the price to drop.

Trend Radar

We watch what is gaining traction before it hits everyone's feed, so the verdict is ready the moment you start wondering.

Reader-First, Always

If a product is bad, we say so even when it would be easy to stay quiet. Your trust is worth more than any single review.
