About the Academy

We teach the skillmost musicians are never taught.

Sight Reading Academy exists for one purpose - to turn the slow, anxious work of decoding a score into the quiet confidence of simply playing it. Fluency is not a gift reserved for the talented. It is a built skill, and this is where it is built.

Why We Exist

Most musicians stall at the same wall.

Their eyes move note by note while the music moves measure by measure.

Every glance becomes a small act of decoding, and decoding is slow. The page wins, the tempo collapses, and confidence quietly erodes. Worse, the player concludes they simply lack a gift - when what they actually lack is a method.

Sight Reading Academy was founded to dismantle that wall. We treat reading the way a conservatory treats technique: as a discipline broken into trainable parts, practiced deliberately, and compounded over time. Intervals become shapes. Rhythms become vocabulary. Harmony becomes the context that lets you anticipate a phrase before your eyes arrive.

The result is not magic. It is the reliable, repeatable confidence of opening a score you have never seen and reading it at tempo.
