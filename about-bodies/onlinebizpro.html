About OnlineBiz Pro

The operator's playbook for building online.

OnlineBiz Pro is straight talk on building, scaling and exiting online businesses. No guru theatrics, no vanity metrics, no course funnel hiding behind the advice - just operating experience, shared with the numbers attached.

Why This Exists

Most online business advice is written to sell you something.

This is written by someone who has built and sold real companies - and shows you the math behind every claim.

Search "how to build an online business" and you get a wall of content engineered to funnel you toward a $2,000 course. The screenshots are cherry-picked, the timelines are fantasy, and the person teaching has usually never run the thing they are teaching.

OnlineBiz Pro was built as the antidote. Every framework here comes from businesses that were actually built, operated and sold - content sites, SaaS tools, productized services. When we talk about pricing, hiring or churn, there is a real P&L behind the words.

The goal is not motivation. It is to compress years of expensive mistakes into guidance you can act on - so you build something durable, profitable, and capable of running without you in the room.

How We Operate

Three rules every article follows

These are the editorial standards that separate operator content from guru content.

Numbers, not hype

Every claim comes with the math - margins, CAC, churn, multiples. If we cannot show the numbers, we do not make the claim.

Built from experience

Frameworks come from businesses actually operated and exited - not from interviewing someone who once read about it.

Honest about the hard parts

We cover the failed launches and the slow quarters too. A playbook that only shows wins is just marketing.
