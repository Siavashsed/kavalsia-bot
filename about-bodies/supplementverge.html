Why This Site Exists

The gap between the label and the literature

The supplement industry is built on confident claims. Bold packaging, proprietary blends, and language engineered to sound clinical without committing to anything a researcher could check. For the person trying to spend money well, it is almost impossible to tell a genuinely useful compound from an expensive placebo.

SupplementVerge was created to close that gap. Every ingredient on this site is read against the human clinical evidence behind it - the randomized controlled trials, the sample sizes, the effect sizes - and assigned a plain letter grade from A to D. The grade is the verdict. Nothing else gets a vote.

We take no sponsorships and run no sponsored reviews. That independence is not a marketing line; it is the only reason a rating here means anything.

// A Multiple high-quality RCTs confirm the effect  ·  B Promising evidence with limitations  ·  C Weak or inconsistent data  ·  D No meaningful human evidence

How We Grade

The same four steps, every single time

No shortcuts for trending ingredients, no special treatment for sponsors - because we have none.

Step 01

PubMed Search

A systematic search filtered for human RCTs. Animal and in-vitro studies never count toward the grade.

Step 02

Quality Assessment

We weigh sample size, blinding, placebo controls, and funding source before anything else.

Step 03

Effect Size Analysis

Statistically significant is not the same as meaningful. We report whether the effect actually matters.

Step 04

Grade Assignment

A final letter reflects the whole body of evidence, and it is updated when new trials publish.
