About InsightInsure

Insurance, explained by someone with no quota to hit.

InsightInsure exists to do one thing well: tell you what insurance companies would rather you not understand - in plain language, with nobody's commission riding on the answer.

Why this site exists

Every piece of consumer insurance content worth reading seemed to be missing. What you could find online was either written by someone paid by an insurer, or so shallow it could not help you make a real decision. Meanwhile, ordinary people kept getting burned by fine print they never read and exclusions nobody bothered to explain.

InsightInsure was built to close that gap. The analysis here is independent, unsponsored, and written for the person trying to make the right call on a car, home, life, or health policy - not for the carrier trying to sell one.

"Policies are written to confuse. Reading them shouldn't require 18 years in the industry - but understanding them sometimes does."

The standards behind every article

1

Independent by design

No carrier sponsorships, no commission incentives. When we name a problem with how a policy is sold, nobody upstream gets a say.

2

Plain language, always

If a clause matters, we translate it. Jargon is the industry's tool, not ours - every guide is written to be understood on the first read.

3

The fine print, surfaced

Exclusions, waiting periods, and coverage gaps get the same space as the headline benefits, because that is where claims are won and lost.
