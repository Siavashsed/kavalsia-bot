About Mochapoo Pets

Mochapoo Pets is a veterinary-grade resource for people who love their dogs and want the truth about caring for them. Every guide here is written or reviewed by a practicing veterinarian - no breeder marketing, no recycled blog posts, no advice shaped by anyone with something to sell.

Why We Exist

It started with the same preventable problem, over and over.

For years, the founders of Mochapoo Pets watched loving, attentive owners walk into the clinic with issues that never had to happen. The cause was almost never neglect. It was confident, well-packaged advice from people whose real goal was a sale - a supplement, a feeding plan, a puppy.

So we built a different kind of resource. Every guide here is grounded in clinical experience and peer-reviewed research, and when the evidence is thin or contradictory, we say so out loud instead of hiding it behind a tidy headline.

The name is our brand, nothing more. What it stands for is simple: calm, accurate guidance for your dog or puppy, written the way a good vet would explain it if they had unlimited time to sit with you.

Evidence over tradition

The Veterinarian Behind It

Dr. Emily Ross, DVM

Founder & Lead Veterinary Editor, Mochapoo Pets

Dr. Ross has spent 14 years in clinical practice, and Mochapoo Pets grew directly out of that work. After thousands of exam-room conversations, she saw how much avoidable worry came from bad information rather than bad owners.

She founded this site to give that consultation-quality guidance to far more people than a single clinic could ever reach. Every article is written or reviewed against her standard: accurate, current, and honest about what is still uncertain.
