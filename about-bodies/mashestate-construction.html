Why We Built This

Most build disasters start at the planning table.

They are rarely caused by a falling beam. They are caused by the skipped permit, the lowball bid that was never going to hold, and the vague contract nobody read closely. By the time the problem shows up on site, the homeowner has already lost their leverage.

MashEstate Construction was built to hand that leverage back. We break the entire build process into plain language so you can spot the warning signs while you can still do something about them - before the first crew shows up, not after.

We take no builder sponsorships, and we update cost data as the market moves. Advice that is six months stale can cost you thousands at the bargaining table, so we treat freshness as a standard, not a courtesy.
