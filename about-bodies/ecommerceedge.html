Why Ecommerce Edge exists
The Story

After a decade inside DTC brands, one pattern kept repeating. Founders were drowning in ecommerce content, yet almost none of it survived contact with a profit-and-loss statement. Blog posts promised "scaling secrets" with no margin math. Agencies sold tactics that ignored CAC. Influencers reviewed tools they were paid to recommend.

Ecommerce Edge was built to be the resource that consultant wished existed: data-driven, unsponsored, and honest about the numbers that actually decide whether a store makes money.

Everything here documents what works in practice - the conversion tests with measured results, the ad structures that survived iOS 14.5, and the unit economics that separate a growing brand from a busy one. If a tactic cannot be defended with data, it does not get published.
