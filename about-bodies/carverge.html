About CarVerge

Automotive coverage that understands how cars actually work.

CarVerge analyzes EVs and modern vehicles the way an engineer would - battery chemistry, real-world range, and cost-of-ownership math, written so a buyer and an engineer can both use it.

Why CarVerge exists

The engineering reality, not the press kit

Most automotive coverage is built around manufacturer talking points and spec sheets that look impressive and explain nothing. A range figure on a brochure is a lab number. A 0-60 time tells you almost nothing about how a battery degrades over five winters.

CarVerge was started to close that gap. Every article works backward from the physics: how the powertrain is built, what the chemistry can sustain, and what it costs to live with. The goal is coverage you can actually make a purchase decision on.

Automotive engineer - 15 years OEM powertrain

Fifteen years designing powertrains at an OEM left me with a very specific allergy: I cannot stand automotive coverage that does not understand how cars actually work. Spec sheets get repeated, marketing claims get printed, and the buyer is left guessing.

CarVerge is the answer to that. Battery chemistry, real-world range data, and cost-of-ownership math - written without a manufacturer relationship to protect. If a vehicle is well engineered, the data will show it. If it is not, the data shows that too.
