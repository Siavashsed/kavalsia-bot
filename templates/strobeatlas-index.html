The deck.

Six things we get asked at the door
FAQ / press / readers
01Are these paid features?+
No. We pay our own door and our own drinks. If we ever cover something where money changed hands, we will say so at the top in mono caps. Promise.
02Can I pitch a night?+
Yes. Drop the line up, the date, the cap, the city and one sentence on why it matters. Pitches go to the newsletter reply address. We read all of them, we cover what we can.
03Do you publish photos of the crowd?+
Rarely, and never without consent. Most of our gallery shots are crowd silhouettes, light, room geometry, or the dancers who actively asked to be shot. The dancefloor stays the dancefloor.
04How do you choose cities?+
We follow the rooms that take risks. A new venue, a strange line up, a residency that refuses to be safe. Big festivals already have enough cameras pointed at them.
05What is in the newsletter?+
A weekly guest list. Five to seven nights we think are worth your time, with the door price, the closing time, and a one line read on the vibe. Sent every Thursday at 4pm UTC.
06Who runs Strobe Atlas?+
Independent. Founded by Riv Solano in 2024. Self funded by reader subscriptions and a small ring of cultural sponsors who have agreed never to influence the writing.
