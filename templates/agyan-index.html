02 / HOUSES

The twelve houses.

The astrology track at Agyan does not begin with sun signs. It begins with houses, the twelve domains that map a life: where you stand, what you hold, how you communicate, what you build. Each house is a doorway to a single sustained question. The Agyan astrology curriculum is twelve lessons because the chart is twelve rooms. We walk one room at a time.

A chart is not a map; it is a language one can listen to.
House I The Body You Arrived In How do you appear before you speak? Studied in AS-01
House II The Things You Carry What do you consider yours, and why? Studied in AS-02
House III The Voice in the Room What did you learn first, and who taught it? Studied in AS-03
House IV The Roof Over Your Memory Where do you go when you close your eyes and feel safe? Studied in AS-04
House V The Things You Make for Joy What do you do that no one is paying you for? Studied in AS-05
House VI The Hands You Use Every Day What work makes you a craftsman, not a worker? Studied in AS-06
House VII The Mirror Across From You Who do you become in the presence of another? Studied in AS-07
House VIII The Door You Have Not Opened What inheritance, debt, or grief are you carrying for someone else? Studied in AS-08
House IX The Distance You Travel In Your Mind What belief have you held that turned out to be a doorway? Studied in AS-09
House X The Name You Are Building What would you like written on the door of the room you walk into? Studied in AS-10
House XI The Circle You Belong To Who would notice if you stopped showing up? Studied in AS-11
House XII The Quiet Behind the Quiet What part of you only speaks when you are alone? Studied in AS-12
03 / FOUR DISCIPLINES

Four disciplines of practice.

Four tracks, twelve lessons each, taught in cohorts and drawn from primary sources. The first three lessons of every track are open to all; lessons four through twelve are reserved for admitted students of the annual program.

M · MEDITATION

Meditation

Sitting practice through breath, posture, and repetition. Twelve lessons on the body as antenna and silence as technique.

MA · MANIFESTATION

Manifestation

Manifestation as a discipline of attention, not magical thinking. Intention, embodiment, action, surrender. Twelve lessons on what desire actually is, and the gravity of what you tend.

The chart as a map, not a forecast. Twelve houses as rooms of a life. Sun, Moon, and the Ascendant as the three doors.

Affirmation

04 / BREATH

Begin with the breath.

INHALE

The breath orb is the simplest practice in the curriculum. Anyone can begin here, today, in two minutes. One slow inhale, one held attention. From this, the body builds its own language.

For sleep or after tension.
For focus and cognitive work.
For long sitting and dhikr.

06 / BECOME A STUDENT

Become a student.

Enrollment at Agyan is not a transaction; it is a small commitment to attention. Three letters per month: long reads, no promotions, no automated sequences. The first letter arrives within two days. Admitted students gain access to all forty-eight lessons, monthly group sessions, and a private reading list curated by Avin and the resident faculty.

You can unsubscribe from any letter. We never sell or share addresses.
