The Philosophy

A Studio Within the Dalmend Collective

Dalmend Home is the residential interiors studio of the Dalmend Collective - a family of design practices devoted to the craft of living well. Where the Collective shapes spaces of every scale, our studio gives its full attention to one thing: the high-end house and the mansion.

We believe true luxury is not loud. It is the depth of a hand-rubbed walnut panel, the weight of a brass door pull, the way late light falls across honed marble. Restraint, material honesty and a sense of occasion guide every room we touch.

"A great house is not decorated. It is composed - room by room, like movements in a quiet symphony."

From the entrance hall that sets the tone to the private library that ends the day, we curate interiors meant to be lived in for generations - not refreshed every season.

Olivia Grant Founding Designer

Maison Lavelle  ·  The Long Gallery
House Study No. 07

A Gallery That Holds the Light

"We wanted a room that would never tire. So we built it from honed limestone, oak with the grain still visible, and one long line of brass."

Set within a nineteenth-century mansion on the southern coast, the long gallery had to do two things at once: receive guests with quiet ceremony and hold the family's growing collection of works on paper. The brief asked for restraint. The architecture asked for a single, generous gesture. We answered with both.

Estate Series Heritage Restoration Olivia Grant
