Why we exist

Pet advice should come from the exam room, not the marketing department

MochaPoo Pets started after years of watching loving owners arrive at the clinic with the same preventable problems. The cause was rarely neglect. It was confident, well-packaged advice from people selling something.

So we built a different kind of resource. Every guide here is grounded in clinical experience and peer-reviewed research - and when the evidence is thin or contradictory, we say that out loud instead of hiding it behind a tidy headline.

Evidence over tradition.

If a popular practice does not hold up in the data, we will tell you - and explain what does.

Plain language, no fear.

Clear guidance you can act on today, written for real owners, not other vets.

Independent and honest.

No breeder partnerships and no pay-to-recommend deals shaping what you read.

What makes us different

Care guidance you can actually trust

Every article on MochaPoo Pets is built to give dog and puppy owners calm, accurate answers - the kind you would get from a good vet who has time to explain.

The person reading every word before you do

Veterinarian & Founding Author
Dr. Emily Ross, DVM
Practicing Veterinarian & Canine Nutrition Specialist

I have spent 14 years in general practice, and most of my days are still spent with dogs and puppies and the owners who adore them. Over and over I met people doing their best with advice that was simply wrong - confident, well-marketed, and not supported by the science.

MochaPoo Pets is my answer to that. Everything published here reflects peer-reviewed research and what I genuinely see work in the exam room. If something does not hold up, I will say so plainly. You deserve the same honest guidance I give my own clients.
