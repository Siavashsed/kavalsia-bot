Timeless Craftsmanship

Built to be inherited, not replaced.

Reference 01 is assembled and regulated by hand at a small atelier in the Vallée de Joux. Every movement passes a five-position timing test before it leaves the workshop. The case is brushed by hand. The crystal is double-domed sapphire with anti-reflective coating applied to the inside surface so the dial reads clean in any light.

Quality You Can Trust

A movement worth servicing.

The ETA 2824-2 inside Reference 01 has been the workhorse of Swiss watchmaking for over fifty years. It is the same caliber a thousand independent restorers can service in any city in the world. Owning Reference 01 means owning a watch you can pass on, not a sealed appliance you throw away when the battery dies.
