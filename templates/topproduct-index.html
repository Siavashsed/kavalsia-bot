Why We Exist

The viral economy was built to empty your wallet.

A single 15 second clip can turn an unknown gadget into a global obsession overnight. The problem is that going viral has nothing to do with being good. Most TikTok-made-me-buy-it products are generic items pulled from the same overseas catalogs, marked up three to five times, and shipped slowly in plain packaging.

TopProduct exists to break that cycle. We buy products with our own money, live with them for at least 30 days, and report exactly what holds up and what falls apart. No affiliate pressure, no brand deals dictating the verdict.

When a viral find is genuinely excellent we will be the first to tell you. When it is recycled hype, we will save you the disappointment and the return shipping.

Good Questions

Frequently asked questions.

How does TopProduct make money if reviews are not sponsored?

We may earn a small commission when readers buy through some links, but that never changes a verdict. We recommend skip-it products constantly, and a brand can never pay to improve its score or position.

What is the Trending Product Scorecard for?

It is a free, instant gut check. When you spot a viral product, run it through the 10 point checklist on this page. The live score tells you whether the basics of a smart purchase are in place before you spend a cent.

How do you decide which products to test?

We track what is trending across social platforms and search every week, then prioritize the products readers ask about most. If something is blowing up your feed, it is probably already on our test bench.

Can I suggest a product for review?

Yes, reader requests are one of our best sources of test ideas. Head to our About page to find the best way to reach the editorial desk with your suggestion.

How often do you publish new reviews?

New reviews and buying guides go up regularly as testing rounds wrap. Joining the weekly digest is the easiest way to get every new verdict the moment it is live.
