The Operator's Approach

Most online businesses do not fail because the product is bad. They fail because the owner is the product.

When every decision, every sale, and every fix runs through you, growth has a hard ceiling - and the business is worth almost nothing the day you stop showing up. That is not a business, it is a demanding job you created for yourself.

OnlineBiz Pro is about the opposite path: building something that compounds while you sleep, hires well, and could be handed off or sold. The chart on the right is a real client - same product, same market, different structure.
