Why CarVerge exists

Most car coverage is hype or fear. Neither helps you decide.

Manufacturer press kits promise the world. Comment sections promise the opposite. Both skip the part that actually matters: how the car performs, holds up and costs in the real world.

CarVerge was built to close that gap. Every claim here traces back to test data, service records, or ownership math you can check yourself. If the numbers do not support a conclusion, we do not publish it.

  • Buying advice built on reliability and resale data, not marketing
  • Maintenance and repair guidance that cuts real shop bills
  • Cost of ownership run over the full lifespan, not the sticker

How we work

Engineering rigor, written to be read

CarVerge treats every article like a bench report: a clear question, measured data, and an honest conclusion you can act on.

Every figure comes from instrumented testing or published cell data. Where a number is an estimate, we say so and show the assumptions.
