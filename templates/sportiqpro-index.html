Quick Wins

6 Performance Tips You Can Use Today

Small, evidence-backed adjustments that pay off fast. No equipment or budget required.

01
Endurance
Keep Easy Days Truly Easy

Most athletes train their easy sessions too hard, blunting recovery. Cap easy runs and rides at 70 percent of max heart rate so hard days stay hard.

~80% of volume should be low intensity
02
Nutrition
Hit a Protein Floor Every Meal

Muscle protein synthesis maxes out around 0.4g per kg of body weight per meal. Spread protein across 3 to 4 meals instead of one big hit.

Aim for 1.6 to 2.2g/kg daily
03
Recovery
Protect Your Sleep Window

Sprint power and reaction time drop sharply after a poor night. Set a fixed bedtime and kill bright screens 60 minutes before to deepen sleep.

Athletes need 8 to 9 hours
04
Strength
Leave 1 to 2 Reps in the Tank

Training near failure on every set adds fatigue without much extra growth. Stop most working sets with a couple of clean reps still available.

RIR 1 to 3 drives most gains
05
Readiness
Check HRV Before You Push

A morning HRV well below your baseline is a signal to swap intensity for an easy session. Let the data, not your ego, set the day's load.

Track a 7-day rolling average
06
Mindset
Rehearse the Effort First

Spend 5 minutes visualizing a hard session or event in vivid detail. Mental rehearsal measurably improves execution and reduces pre-start nerves.

Used by Olympic-level athletes
