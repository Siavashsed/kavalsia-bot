Chapter 04 / Ignition

Tips that land tomorrow.

Small, evidence-backed adjustments that pay off fast. No equipment, no budget, no waiting for next Monday.

01
Endurance
Keep easy days truly easy

Most athletes train their easy sessions too hard, blunting recovery. Cap easy efforts at 70 percent of max heart rate so hard days stay hard.

~80% of volume at low intensity
02
Recovery
Protect your sleep window

Sprint power and reaction time drop sharply after a poor night. Lock a fixed bedtime and kill bright screens 60 minutes before to deepen sleep cycles.

Athletes need 8 to 9 hours
03
Strength
Leave 1 to 2 reps in the tank

Training near failure on every set adds fatigue without much extra growth. Stop most working sets with a couple of clean reps still available - then come back stronger.

RIR 1 to 3 drives most gains
