Month-by-Month Milestones

What to expect - and when to stop comparing. A realistic guide to your baby's first year that celebrates range, not checkboxes.

Read the Full Guide
Months 0-1
Responds to voice, tracks movement with eyes, irregular sleep of 16-18hrs
Months 2-3
Social smiles begin, recognizes faces, sleeps up to 5hrs in a stretch
Months 4-6
Rolls over, laughs out loud, reaches for objects, ready for solids
Months 7-12
Sits independently, first words emerging, separation anxiety peaks
