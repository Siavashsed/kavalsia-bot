Our Process

How every article earns
its place on Nexus.

We hold every article in the network to the same four-stage editorial standard before it appears in the feed.

01
Expert Authorship

Every article must be authored by a credentialed specialist - a certified practitioner, licensed professional, or domain expert with demonstrable experience in their niche. We do not publish generalist content.

02
Source Verification

Factual claims must be backed by primary or peer-reviewed sources. Editorial staff cross-reference key statistics, medical claims, financial figures, and any data presented as fact before publication.

03
Style and Clarity Review

All content is reviewed for adherence to AP Style guidelines. We flag ambiguous language, unsupported claims, sensationalist framing, or misleading headlines before anything goes live.

04
Ongoing Accuracy Audits

Published content is periodically reviewed and updated as new data or research emerges. Articles that no longer reflect current best practices are updated, flagged, or removed.
